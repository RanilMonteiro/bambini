Our Little Story

Bambini is a registered, family-owned preschool that has been established for 36 years. Bambini was originally started by Jenny du Plessis and now has been taken over by her daughter's Jacqui and Leigh. Bambini Preschool accepts children from 18 months to 6 years. Bambini has 4 classes; the Playgroup class, the Grade 000 class, the Grade 00 class and the Grade R class. Bambini also offers Aftercare for children in Grade 1 to Grade 7.
